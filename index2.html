Starting a new Legacy

A new legacy is born this year from the cracks of the old one. We are all set to repeat the history in a whole new format, Bringing in ZEST’17 “Fiesta De Technico” and so is the new life to the old soul and merging in renowned professors, esteemed delegates, prestigious alumni, eminent personalities and distinguished social activist.

Our History

Earlier, each Engineering Department organised their techno-cultural fest separately namely- Nirmaan, Mecharnival, Resonance, Incord and Techera. Each of these fests based their events on their respective trades and gained success in parts. The think-tank pondered upon raking in the moolah generated by the fests on an individual scale into an integrated Technical Festival of the University, Zest. The School of Engineering has come up with the Technical Festival, Zest that functions on the joint efforts of all the engineering branches into one force that will generate grand success on the Technical front. The zealous effort of the students is set to create a Fest of magnanimous scale and is definitely poised to be full of energy and grandeur.

Opportunity

This fest is a perfect platform which not only acts as a breeding ground for technical, entrepreneurial and social ideas but also gauges one’s leadership, management and execution skills, making this Carnival a synergy of Technical Growth and personality development.

Technical Aspects

: It is always preferred to implement a sustainable solution to a technical problem that strives to solve it for infinite future, synergistically. Engineers processes holistically, use system analysis and integrate environmental impact assessment tools while preserving biodiversity and respecting all life forms.

Financial Consideration

Maintaining a positive genuine long- term investment considering all types of capital is the key to answer, “How much can we save?” and try to know our “needs” and “wants” ,primarily focusing on achieving needs of larger mass.

Social Impacts

Sustainability must offer individuals and communities the opportunity to increase their capabilities , ensuring that all material and energy inputs and outputs are as inherently safe and benign as possible, while enhancing the social cohesion and quality of life we live.
